About CoDevX

We started small.
We build big.

CoDevX is an Egyptian software technology company with a global vision. We partner with startups, established companies, and even freelancers to turn ideas into functional software—quickly, efficiently, and without administrative complexities.

Our team is small by design. Every project gets senior attention, direct communication, and code that's actually maintainable after we hand it over.

Our Story

A quick timeline.

2024

The Idea

two developers fed up with over-engineered agencies decided to build something leaner. CoDevX was sketched on a whiteboard in Ismailia.

2025

First Clients

We completed five projects in our first year—mostly for clients on freelance platforms, and most with tight deadlines. We delivered them all on time.

2026

Going Wider

Our work has expanded to include mobile applications, and we've added user interface and user experience design to our expertise. We've also helped build a real website for a startup organization, and we've worked as part of a cohesive team.

Now

Building CoDevX

Now we have decided to have our own company to provide better and faster services and to reach customers more flexibly.
